sea painting
The particle line generated by the digital algorithm is driven by the real sea water image. The moving direction and speed of the particle line are affected by the wave direction of the real sea water. The color change information of the particle is collected from the color of the real sea wave. Digital algorithms link physical and virtual worlds.
