: About me

Biography

Li Jing, visual director, founder and artistic director of digital Galaxy (Beijing) culture media Co., Ltd., is committed to creating immersive new media environment, interactive art installations and audio-visual performances. Explore the multidimensional nature of human experience by creating works at the intersection of media art, physical space and virtual machine intelligence. He has been invited to serve as visual director and multimedia design director in large-scale art festival exhibitions and stage performances at home and abroad for many times. His representative works include: immersive experience cultural tourism real scene show "man Xiu", magic real scene immersive light and shadow show "Legend of the sea of heaven", acrobatic drama "dream back to Zhongshan", acrobatic drama "Legend of lion awakening", dance drama "Taibai Wanyun", international light and shadow art season "symbiosis of all things" art installation exhibition, etc.
